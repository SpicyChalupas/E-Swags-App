Rewarding Student Participation In Campus Improvements

LU-Bucks turns your ideas into campus impact

LU-Bucks is a participation platform for Lewis University students and staff. Create an account, share feedback through surveys and event/improvement ideas, earn submission credits, and use those credits for weekly and monthly raffle entries.

How it works

  1. Create your LU-Bucks account.
  2. Submit surveys and share ideas for events or university improvements.
  3. Earn credits and enter weekly and monthly raffles.

Your choice: named or anonymous

Want your feedback tied to your profile? You can do that. Prefer privacy? You can submit anonymously when sharing ideas.

The goal is simple: make it easy for everyone in the Lewis community to contribute honestly and consistently.

Why this matters at Lewis University

Better campus experiences come from steady input. LU-Bucks helps turn student and staff perspectives into meaningful improvements while rewarding engagement along the way.

  • Boosts participation in surveys and campus feedback.
  • Creates a central place for event and improvement ideas.
  • Rewards consistent contributors with raffle opportunities.

Ready to participate?

Log in or register for an account to start earning credits and entering raffles.
